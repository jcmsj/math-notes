# Quantiles
## Types
1. __Quantile | Q__ - 4 equal parts.
2. __Decile | D__ - 10 equal parts.
3. __Percentile | P__ - 100 equal parts.
## Ungrouped formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\cfrac{k}{N}(n+1) \\
&\text{where:} \\
k &= \text{target quantile} \\
N &= \text{type} \\
n &= \text{data size}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example
$$
\begin{aligned}
Q_1 = \dfrac{1}{4}(n+1)
\end{aligned}
$$
