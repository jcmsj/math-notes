## Pythagorean Identity
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sinh^2 u + \cosh^2 u &= 1 \\
1+\coth^2 u &= csch^2 u \\
\tanh^2 u +1  &= sech^2 u
\end{aligned}
$$

## Double Angle
$\sinh 2 u = 2 \sinh u \cosh u$ <br>
$\cosh 2 u = \cosh^2 u + \sinh^2 u$

## Euler
$e^u = \cosh u + \sinh u$ <br>
$e^{-u} = \cosh u - \sinh u$

$\sinh u = \cfrac{e^u - e^{-u}}{2}$ <br>
$\cosh u = \cfrac{e^u + e^{-u}}{2}$
