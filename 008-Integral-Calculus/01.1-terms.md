# Antiderivative
$$
\begin{aligned}
* \;f(x)\; is\; any\; F(x)\; if\; \frac{d}{dx}F(x) = f(x)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
where:&\\
\int\; &- Integral\; Sign \\
f(x)\; &- \;Integrand \\
x\; &- \; variable \; of \; integration \\
F(x)\;+\;c \; &- \; Indefinite\; integral \\
c \; &- \;Constant \; of \; integration \\
\end{aligned}
$$

* __Antiderivation__ - Reverses differentiation
