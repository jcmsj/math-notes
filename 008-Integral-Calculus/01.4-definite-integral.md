## Definite Integral
* A function's area under a curve
$$
\int^{b}_{a}\;f(x)\;dx = \int\,f(b) - \int\,f(a)\
$$

* Swapping the limits <br>
$$
\int^{b}_{a}\;f(x)\;dx = -\int^{a}_{b}\;f(x)\;dx
$$
* u-substitution <br>
$$
\begin{aligned}
\int^{b}_{a}\;f(x)\;dx &= \\ 
\int^{u(b)}_{u(a)}\;u\;du &= \int\,f(b) - \int\,f(a)\
\end{aligned}
$$
