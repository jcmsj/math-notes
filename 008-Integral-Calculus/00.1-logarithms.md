* Log of 1: <br>
$\log_a 1 = 0$

* Logarithm of the base: <br>
$\log_a a = 1$

* Power rule: <br>
$\log_a x^n = n \log_a x$

* Base change: <br>
$\log_b a = \dfrac{\log_x a}{\log_x b}$

* Product rule: <br>
$\log_a (x \cdot y) = \log_a x + \log_a y$

* Quotient rule: <br>
$\log_a \dfrac{x}{y} = \log_a x - \log_a y$

* Log of 0 an   d -n: <br>
* $\log_a(0 \; or -n) =  undefined$
